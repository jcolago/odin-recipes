Spinach Tofu Scramble

Description

A healthy and versatile dish to start your day! Any vegetables can be used in place of the spinach for a flavorful punch!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 lb. firm or extra firm tofu packed in water, drained well
  • 1 tsp. nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste
  • 1/4 tsp. ground turmeric
  • 4 cups loosely packed fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat a medium skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil; when it ripples, add the onion. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is soft, 3 to 5 minuets.
  2. Use fringers to crumble the drained tofu into bite-sized pieces. Add to skillet.
  3. Add the nutritional yeast, salt, pepper, and turmeric. Stir to combine. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the tofu is hot, about 3 minuets.
  4. Add spinach and cook until wilted, about 1 minute. Sprinkle the lemon juice over the scramble. Season with addition salt and pepper to taste.
